Blueberry Cake

Description

The orgins of this scrumptious, dense - just the right amount of sweet - cake recipe comes from the quirky Food Network hit “Good Eats”. Altered to personal taste, I have made it my own and is now expected at every family holiday.

This recipe makes a 9x9 inch cake.

Ingredients

For Cake

  • 2 Cups Cake Flour (but I'm not fancy so I use regular flour)
  • 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 2 Oz Softened Butter (I like mine salty)
  • 3/4 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 3 Cups Blueberries

For Topping

  • 2 Oz Softened Butter
  • 1/3 Cup Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Hand mix flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. With mixer, blend on medium speed butter, sugar, egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Reduce speed to low and alternate adding dry mixture and milk, a little at a time until well combined.
  5. Fold in blueberries.
  6. Pour batter into well greased and floured 9x9 inch pan.
  7. For the topping, in seperate bowl combine butter flour sugar and cinnamon into a crumb mixture using finger tips or a fork.
  8. Sprinkle topping evenly across to cake batter.
  9. Cook in oven for about 35 minutes.
