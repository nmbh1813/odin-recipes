Smoothies

Concocted by me when I started to feel like a bad mother who didn't feed her child enough veggies! So delicious it's hard to believe that it is healthy!

Makes 2 small smoothies. Or one big one!

TIP: When your fruits (and some veggies) look like they're about to go bad and you know you're not going to be able to eat it all -- chop it up and freeze it for smoothies!

Ingredients

  • 1 Frozen Banana (either cut in half or chunks)
  • 1/2 Cup Frozen Strawberries
  • 1/4 Cup Frozen Blueberries
  • 1/4 Cup Frozen (uncooked) Spinach
  • About 4 Baby Carrots
  • 1 Yogurt Cup (Flavor of choice - We like vanilla or berry)
  • About 1/2 Cup Milk
  • About 1/2 Cup Fruit Juice (Flavor of choice - We like cranberry)

Directions

  1. Put all of it in a blender.
  2. Blend thoroughly.
  3. If not blending well, add a little more milk or juice at a time until bleands together.
  4. Pour into cups and enjoy!
