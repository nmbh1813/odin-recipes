Banana Bread

Description

WARNING: The following recipe is TOP SECRET.

This beautiful recipe has been perfected by the brilliant and lovely Grandma Anna. Follow it to a “T” and you will never experience dry banana bread ever again. Still tastes amazing even if you burn it.

This recipe makes 2 loaves.

Ingredients

  • 5 Cups Flour
  • 4 Cups Sugar
  • 7 Tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 1/3 Cup Milk
  • 6 Tbsp Canola Oil
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Cups Chopped Nuts
  • 2 or More Extra Ripe Bananas (mashed)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In large bowl mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  3. Add all wet ingredients (milk, canola oil, eggs, mashed bananas) to dry mixture.
  4. Mix gently until all ingredients are well combined. Be sure not to over mix.
  5. Pour mixture into well greased and floured loaf pans.
  6. Cook in oven for about 1 hour.
